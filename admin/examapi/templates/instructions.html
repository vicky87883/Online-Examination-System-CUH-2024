General Instructions

  1. The clock will be set at the server. The countdown timer at the top right corner of the screen will display the remaining time for you to complete the examination.
  2. The Question Palette displayed on the right side of the screen will show the status of each question.
  3. You can navigate to a specific question by clicking on the corresponding question number in the Question Palette.
  4. Choose the correct option from the multiple choices and click 'Save & Next' to save your answer.
  5. Once you have answered a question, you can move to the next one.
